Debit card is also known as ATM card, plastic card, check card or bank card that can be used instead of cash at the time of purchasing. A debit card is a payment card that deducts money directly from a consumer's checking account to pay for a purchase. It eliminate the need to carry cash or physical checks to make purchases directly from your savings. It is the most convenient and secure method to say no to cash. With the help of debit card do online payments, balance enquiry and also can generate mini-statements from an ATM.
